Cancellation Policy

At Pink Day Tour and Travels, we understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent. Please review the following timelines and refund details carefully.

Cancellation Timelines and Refund

Cancellation Period Refund Amount
60 days pre-travel date 70% of total package cost
59-40 days pre-travel date 20% of total package cost
39-25 days pre-travel date 25% of total package cost
24-15 days pre-travel date 15% of total package cost
Less than 20 days No Refund

Important Notes

  • All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.
  • Non-refundable components (e.g., permits, tickets) will be deducted from the refund amount.
  • In case of unforeseen circumstances (e.g., natural disasters), special considerations may apply.
